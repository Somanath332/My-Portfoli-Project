Experimental Investigation on Solar Dryer by Forced Convection

Objective:

This project presents the design and construction of a solar dryer by Forced Convection. The dryer is composed of solar collector, a fan and a solar drying chamber constraining rack of three trays both being integrated together. The air allowed in through air inlet is heated up in the solar collector and channeled through the drying chamber where it is utilized in drying. The design and data were obtained for proper design specification. Locally available material were used for the construction, namely comprising of wood, glass, aluminum metal sheet, fan and net trays. The rapid rate of drying by forced convection in the dryer as compared to drying with natural convection revels its ability to dry food items reasonable rapid pace to remove moisture.
